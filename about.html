My name is Amelia, I am a multifaceted freelance and graphic artist. My work has ranged from logo and company design to pallets and original characters for use in DnD and MMORPGs, also for use in novels and fanfictions. I have created art and designs for youtube channels, games and novels. I currently work as a logo designer for a Facebook page and group, to design their profile images and banners, and as the graphic designer for AmeCat Games. While most of my work is in pixels or pencil, I dabble in every artistic medium I can find. I currently am experimenting in watercolors and ink.

To learn about cost of commissions and to ask any questions,hit that contact button. I'll be happy to assist! Thanks for stopping by, enjoy Amethyst Art
